Disinformation Identifier

I worked with a small team of programmers to develop an algorithm to identify and respond to disinformation campaigns on social media sites. It was abale to identify disinformation in text as well as in images and videos by using an OCR model.

Lyme Disease Detector

Learn more!

Lyme Disease Detector

I developed a mobile app capable of identiying the characteristic Erythema Migrans (EM) rash, which is a key symptom of Lyme disease. This rash can look like a number of other similar rashes, which means that doctors only identify it correctly 40% of the time. This model, however, is much better (90% accuracy.)
